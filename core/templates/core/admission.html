2025 School Fees Structure

At Paterson Park Primary School, we strive to provide quality Christian education at affordable rates. Our fee structure is designed to cover essential educational costs while remaining accessible to our community.

View School Fees


×

Payment Options

  • Annual payment: 5% discount if paid in full by 31 January 2025
  • Termly payments: Due on the first day of each term
  • Monthly payments: Due by the 7th of each month (January to November)
  • Sibling discount: 10% discount for second child, 15% for third and subsequent children

Important Notes:

School fees do not include textbooks, stationery, uniforms, or extracurricular activities. A separate fee schedule is available for aftercare services and optional activities. Financial assistance may be available for qualifying families - please contact the finance office for more information.
